Privacy Policy for Arianimal

Last updated: August 1, 2025

This App is a mobile fan companion app for superfans. This app is built to be fun, lightweight, and privacy-first. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users.

1. No Account, No Tracking

  • You do not need to register or log in to use this app.
  • We do not ask for your name, email, location, or any personal information.

2. Anonymous by Design

  • We do not store user profiles, device identifiers, or behavioral data.
  • We do not use cookies or local storage to track user behavior.

This app uses Google AdMob to serve in-app advertisements. AdMob may collect limited device data to:

  • Prevent fraud and abuse

This data is handled solely by Google and is not accessed or stored by this app.

For more information, visit Google’s policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

If you're located in the European Union or California, you are entitled to specific rights under GDPR and CCPA. While we do not collect personal data ourselves, AdMob may process limited data for advertising purposes. You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings.

5. No Data to Share or Sell

This app can not share or sell any user data — because we do not collect any!
